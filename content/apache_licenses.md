At the bottom of the Apache 2.0 License file, there's an appendix:

    APPENDIX: How to apply the Apache License to your work.

    ...

    Copyright [yyyy] [name of copyright owner]

    ...

Does that look like an invitation to fill in the blanks to you? It sure does to me, and has [for](https://github.com/rust-lang/rust/pull/24068) [others](https://github.com/rust-lang/rust/pull/13391#issuecomment-40270597) in the Rust community as well.

Today I was doing some licensing housekeeping and made the same [embarrassing](https://github.com/rust-lang/regex/pull/117) [mistake](https://github.com/rust-lang/regex/pull/118).

This is a PSA to double check whether inviting blanks are part of the appendix before filling them out in Apache license texts.
